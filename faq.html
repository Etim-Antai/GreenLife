Frequently Asked Questions

What is Telemedicine?

Telemedicine allows you to connect with healthcare professionals remotely via video, phone, or chat, providing convenient access to medical care from anywhere.

How do I book a virtual consultation?

You can book a virtual consultation by signing into your account, selecting a doctor, and choosing an available time slot that fits your schedule.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security by using advanced encryption and security measures to protect your personal health information.

Can I get a prescription through telemedicine?

Yes, our doctors can provide prescriptions based on your consultation, which can be sent directly to your preferred pharmacy.

What if I need urgent care?

Our platform is designed for non-emergency medical consultations. For urgent or life-threatening conditions, please contact your local emergency services immediately.
