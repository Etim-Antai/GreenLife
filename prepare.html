Preparing for Your Virtual Visit

Preparing for your virtual visit is crucial for a smooth and effective consultation. Follow these steps to ensure you are ready:

  • Check your internet connection and ensure your device is compatible with the telemedicine platform.
  • Gather any necessary medical documents or information you may need for your consultation.
  • Set up a quiet, well-lit space where you can have a private conversation with your healthcare provider.
  • Test your microphone and camera before the appointment to avoid technical issues.
  • Have your insurance information and payment details ready if required.
